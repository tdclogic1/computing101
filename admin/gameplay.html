Game Rules

Objective

The objective of the game is to solve the mystery by correctly identifying the suspect, weapon, and location of the crime.

Setup

  • Each player creates an account and logs into the game platform.
  • Players join a game room and choose a character avatar.
  • The system randomly selects a suspect, weapon, and location. These are hidden from the players.
  • The remaining cards are distributed digitally to the players.
  • Each player receives a digital detective notebook to keep track of their clues.

Gameplay

  • Players take turns moving their avatars and making suggestions about the crime.
  • A suggestion must include a suspect, weapon, and location.
  • If the suggestion matches the cards in another player's digital hand, that player must show one of the matching cards to the player who made the suggestion.
  • If the suggestion does not match any cards in another player's hand, it is noted as a potential solution.

Accusation

  • When a player feels confident, they can make an accusation, naming the suspect, weapon, and location.
  • If the accusation is correct, the player wins the game.
  • If the accusation is incorrect, the player is eliminated from making further suggestions or accusations, but must continue showing cards if asked.

Winning the Game

The game is won when a player correctly identifies the suspect, weapon, and location of the crime.
