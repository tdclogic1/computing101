Game: Clue

Research

-author: Jar La

  • The scene is set in a building of choice, most typically a mansion
  • The players are each given roles- one or two people playing as the murderer
  • To win the game of clue you have to find three of the following about the case:
    • Who did it?
    • With what weapon
    • Where
  • The other players ask a clue question
  • There are 6 players in total, the rest are all side characters and the host ends up dead mysteriously
